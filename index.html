About the retreat

A space to slow down
and come home to yourself

At our Bali Yoga Retreat, we believe that healing begins in stillness. Nestled within a private jungle estate in Ubud, each day is designed to gently return you to your body, your breath, and your natural state of ease.

Through carefully woven yoga practices, guided meditation, conscious breathwork, and nourishing Balinese cuisine, you will rediscover what it means to live fully in the present moment. This is not a fitness programme — it is an invitation to heal.

  • ◌ Body awareness
  • ◌ Mindfulness & presence
  • ◌ Healthy conscious living
  • ◌ Connection with nature
What we offer

Nourishing practices
for body & soul

Every activity is chosen to restore, awaken, and inspire — held by experienced guides who honour both ancient tradition and modern understanding.

  • 🌅

    Morning Yoga

    Begin each day with a grounding vinyasa or yin practice as the jungle wakes around you.

  • 🧘

    Meditation

    Daily guided sessions to cultivate stillness, clarity, and a quiet mind amid the tropics.

  • 💨

    Breathwork

    Pranayama and conscious breathing techniques to reset your nervous system and awaken vitality.

  • 🔔

    Sound Healing

    Balinese singing bowls and gong baths to guide you into deep relaxation and inner stillness.

  • 🌊

    Waterfall Trips

    Journey through the jungle to hidden sacred waterfalls for natural immersion and spiritual cleansing.

  • 🌇

    Sunset Beach Yoga

    Flow with the rhythm of the tides as golden light fills the horizon — a practice you will never forget.

  • 🥥

    Healthy Food

    Lovingly prepared plant-based Balinese cuisine using local organic ingredients to nourish from within.

  • ✶

    Body Awareness

    Somatic movement and embodiment practices to help you reconnect deeply with your physical self.

Where we are
